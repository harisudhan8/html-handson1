benifits of vegetable salad

Salad greens contain Vitamin A, Vitamin C, beta-carotene, calcium, folate, fiber, and phytonutrients

steps to make vegetable salad

  • take a large and clear glass bowl.
  • try adding blanched or lightly steamed veggies like broccoli, fresh beans, corn, cauliflower, baby corn.
  • add lentils, beans or chickpeas or bean sprouts for more protein.
  • grate some of the vegitable for different texture.
  • add marinated veggies like artichoke hearts for tang and other veggies like radish for some pugency.
  • swap your own favourite combination of fried fruits, nuts and seeds
  • use fresh coriander and chews instead of using parsley.
